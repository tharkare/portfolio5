wedding pavilion

This wedding pavilion concept is so that in order to get from one space to another, you must pass through the pavilion. This shows the importance of the pavilion to the rest of the spaces, which include a parking area for the wedding party, a viewing garden for the ceremony, and a reception garden for after the ceremony. In the cube itself is a second door where the wedding party may get ready, stairs for the bride to walk down when it is time for the ceremony, and a space for the ceremony to take place. The other two planes are positioned in such a way, where they emphasize the two main spaces of the scheme; the viewing and reception gardens. The entrance to the storage and restroom area are located in the reception area.
